Potato Salad

Image Above: Photography by Rachel Vanni, Food Styling by Simon Andrews.

Potato Salad is a classic, household favourite that is easy to prepare.

This is the perfect dish to bring to a picnic or BBQ this summer.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5kg Washed potatoes, cut into quarters
  • 2/3 cup (200g) vegan mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbs finely chopped chives
  • 4 spring onion, thinly sliced
  • salt and pepper for seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Cook the potato in a large saucepan of boiling water for 10 mins or until tender. Drain well. Set aside to cool.
  2. In a seperate bowl, whisk the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard and half the chives in a small bowl. Add 2-3 tsp water and whisk to combine. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Combine potato and three-quarters of the spring onion in a large bowl. Drizzle with the dressing and gently toss to combine. Transfer to a serving bowl and sprinkle with remaining spring onion and chives.
